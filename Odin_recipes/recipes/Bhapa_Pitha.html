Bhapa Pitha

Description

Bhapa pitha is a popular sweet snack amongst Bengali and Assamese people. It is delicate, soft, and contain various forms of texture with a fusion of rich flavour as you bite into the pitha.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Grated coconut
  • Jaggery
  • Salt
  • Warm water

Steps

  1. Dissolve salt in warm water and set aside.
  2. Mix rice flour water the water.
  3. Cover the rice flour with a towel for 20 minutes
  4. Take a fresh small coconut and shred.
  5. Run the rice flour through a strainer to obtain fine grain texture.
  6. Place the rice flour in a palm size mold cup and gently press down with a spoon.
  7. Add jaggery bits and shredded coconut on top.
  8. Gently cover the jaggery and coconut with more rice flour on top and then wrap it with a wet cheese cloth.
  9. Place the wrapped pitha on top of the pot with water that has been sealed with a cheese cloth for indirect heat.
  10. Cover with a lid and cook the pitha for about 3 minutes until delicately soft.
  11. Remove from heat and repeat the steps until all ingredients have been used up.
  12. Serve pitha warm or cold as desired
